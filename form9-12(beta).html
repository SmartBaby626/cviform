Instructions to Parents/ Guardians
We want to know about what your child is like now. These questions are designed for a range of ages and abilities, so some questions may seem odd or may not be relevant to your child. Your child may have difficulty with some of the activities listed below but not others – this is normal.

You may also notice that your child has more difficulty with some of the activities when they are tired, this is not uncommon.

For each of the items listed on the following pages, please tick the box which best fits with your child’s present behaviour:
never / rarely / sometimes / often / always / not applicable (NA)
